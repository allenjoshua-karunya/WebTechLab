Environmental Pollution – Types and Effects

What is Environmental Pollution?

Environmental pollution refers to the contamination of natural resources by harmful substances. These pollutants affect air, water, soil, and noise levels, leading to health issues and ecological damage.

Types of Pollution

Air Pollution
Caused by vehicle emissions, factories, and burning of fossil fuels.
Water Pollution
Caused by industrial waste, oil spills, and sewage discharge.
Soil Pollution
Caused by pesticides, chemicals, and landfill waste.
Noise Pollution
Caused by traffic, industries, and loudspeakers.

Effects of Pollution

Pollution has serious consequences on both health and the environment:

  1. Health Effects
    1. Respiratory issues
    2. Waterborne diseases
  2. Environmental Damage
    1. Climate change
    2. Loss of biodiversity
  • Air Pollution
    • Smog formation
    • Ozone layer depletion
  • Water Pollution
    • Fish kills
    • Contaminated drinking water
